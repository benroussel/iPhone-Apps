Dreamology Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Dreamology is designed with your privacy as the top priority.

- Dreamology does not collect any personal data.
- Dreamology does not use analytics or tracking of any kind.
- Dreamology does not contain ads.
- Dreamology does not communicate with any server.
- Dreamology does not use any third-party SDKs.
- All data (dream entries, analysis results, settings) is stored exclusively on your device.
- Dream analysis is performed entirely on-device using Apple's NaturalLanguage framework and a bundled rule-based engine. No dream text is ever sent to any server.
- Voice recording for dream dictation uses Apple's on-device speech recognition. Audio is processed locally and is never transmitted.
- Apple Health sleep data is read locally to display alongside dream entries. It is never stored externally or shared.
- Face ID / Touch ID authentication is handled entirely by iOS LocalAuthentication. No biometric data is accessed or stored by the app.
- The optional In-App Purchase is processed by Apple's App Store. Dreamology does not handle or store any payment information.

Dreamology has no backend, no accounts, and no way to access your data. Your dreams are yours alone.
