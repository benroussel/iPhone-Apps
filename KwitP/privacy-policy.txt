KwitP Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

KwitP is designed with your privacy as the top priority.

- KwitP does not collect any personal data.
- KwitP does not use analytics or tracking of any kind.
- KwitP does not contain ads.
- KwitP does not communicate with any server.
- KwitP does not use any third-party SDKs.
- All data (streaks, urges, relapses, check-ins, selfies, settings) is stored exclusively on your device.
- KwitP uses Apple's FamilyControls framework for content filtering. This is handled entirely by iOS and no data leaves your device.
- Selfie photos taken during accountability check-ins are stored locally on your device as JPEG files and are never uploaded or shared unless you explicitly choose to share them.

KwitP has no backend, no accounts, and no way to access your data. Your recovery journey is yours alone.
