Quit To Win Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Quit To Win is designed with your privacy as the top priority.

- Quit To Win does not collect any personal data.
- Quit To Win does not use analytics or tracking of any kind.
- Quit To Win does not contain ads.
- Quit To Win does not communicate with any server.
- Quit To Win does not use any third-party SDKs.
- All data (streaks, cravings, wins, relapses, check-ins, selfies, smoking profile, settings) is stored exclusively on your device.
- Selfie photos taken during accountability check-ins are stored locally on your device as JPEG files and are never uploaded or shared unless you explicitly choose to share them.
- Guided sessions use Apple's built-in text-to-speech engine. No audio data leaves your device.
- In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. Quit To Win does not process or store any payment information.

Quit To Win has no backend, no accounts, and no way to access your data. Your quit journey is yours alone.
